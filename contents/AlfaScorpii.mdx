##### Introduction

Alfa Scorpii CRM is a state-of-the-art platform tailored exclusively for PT Alfa Scorpii. It streamlines business operations through an intuitive interface, robust contact management, and real-time sales tracking. With automated reporting and insightful analytics, it empowers decision-makers to thrive in a dynamic business environment.

##### Project Details

This comprehensive customer relationship management system is engineered to optimize operational efficiency and adapt to evolving business needs. Leveraging efficient contact management, and seamless automated reporting, the platform provides all the tools essential for managing customer interactions and driving sustained growth.

##### Key Features

- **Intuitive Dashboard:** Offers a clear overview of critical performance metrics.
- **Advanced Contact Management:** Organizes customer data for quick access and updates.
- **Real-Time Sales Tracking:** Monitors sales trends and provides actionable insights.
- **Automated Reporting:** Generates detailed reports to support strategic decisions.

##### Technologies Used

- **Framework:** Next.js
- **Programming Language:** TypeScript
- **State Management:** Jotai
- **UI Framework:** Shadcn UI
- **CSS Framework:** Tailwind CSS
